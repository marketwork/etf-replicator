How it works

  1. Pull constituent universe for the ETF (S&P 500 for SPY, NASDAQ-100 for QQQ, iShares/SPDR CSV for sector ETFs)
  2. Download adjusted daily prices for all constituents
  3. Apply selection method to identify N stocks with highest collective replication power
  4. Solve constrained QP: min‖S·w − e‖² subject to Σwᵢ = 1, wᵢ ≥ 0
  5. Report in-sample and out-of-sample tracking error, R², and correlation
